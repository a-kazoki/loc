Translation

Our focus is on providing high quality yet competitive in price translation services that build on globally recognized translation standards, and a strong network of qualified in-house and freelance native-speaker translators located in target countries.

We provide different types of translations:

  • Software localization
  • Multimedia localization
  • Web content localization
  • Legal localization
  • Medical localization

We are ready to support our clients with different combinations of translation sub-services:

  • Translate only
  • Translation + Editing
  • Translation + Editing + Proofreading
